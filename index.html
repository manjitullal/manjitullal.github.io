About me

Tycho Brahe in the 15th century carefully measured the position of planets. Even though his geocentric model of the world was not correct the act of collecting the data through careful observation emphasized the importance of data in Science. Johannes Kepler looked at the Brahe's measurements and saw a pattern. He modelled that the orbit of the planets around the Sun is not in perfect circles but an ellipse. This was a revelation in Physics. Fast forward, we are living in 21st century, where we have abundance of data. All we need is a careful eye for detail and creative imagination to find the patterns in the data.

I am passionate about Data Science, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. I believe every field and every person can leverage AI for their benefit. Not all problems can be solved using AI. AI certainly will unlock new ways of thinking about the problem and arriving at the solution. Feel free to message me on LinkedIn if you have a position or want to understand my work. I am continually updating my skill set and therefore I can bring something new to the table every time, be it professionally or personally.
